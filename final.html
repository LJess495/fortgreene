A Great Time
Fort Greene Park is a great place for fun activities. I enjoy going to the park on a weekend with my sibilings. The last time I went to the park was with my sibilings to catch the sunrise. We woke up super early that morning, walked up the 100 stairs, set the tripod, and waited for the moment. Unfortunately, it was a very cloudy day. When the clouds cleared, the sun was already in mid air. Our idea to take a time-lapse video failed, but we knew better. As we planned the next attempt in our heads, we didn't miss the chance to take have fun at the moment. We took lots of pictures of people, animals, sky, clouds, and anything we could possibly find. The park is very special to me because I had a memorable adventure time there. When I look back at the pictures I took, I can always remember the great time I had with my siblings.

Visit the Park
Fort Greene Park is in the Fort Greene neighborhood by Dekalb Avenue &, S Portland Ave. If you are visiting Brooklyn or lives in Brooklyn, plan your visit to the Fort Greene Park today!
