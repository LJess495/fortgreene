Dog Party

Come visit Fort Greene Park at any time of the day (just maybe not a rainy day - but some dogs absolutely loves the rain) and no doubt, you will find dogs! Big grassfields and lawn spaces perfect for dog owners and dog lovers alike. With plenty of space to run and play, your pups will have a blast making new friends and soaking up the sunshine. As for you, this will be an opportunity to meet other dog friends and chat tips on how to perfect your pet care skills. And if you're someone like me who does not have a pet, then it's time for free dog watching! You can watch how dogs play with each other and use this time to familiarize with different dog species. But the best part for me, is to walk around and wait for dogs to come to me! Then I would as their owner for permission, and before they run away from you, pet them! Get ready for the dog party on your next visit to Fort Greene Park!

Extracurricular

Fort Greene park is an excellent place to indulge in extracurricular activities such as tennis, jogging, walking, and relaxing. Tennis enthusiasts can occupy the tennis courts and polish their skills. If you are lucky enough to see a game happening, take a stop to watch a free match! Joggers can take advantage of the park's walking trails for an early morning or evening routine. With the many entrances and exits, your run will be more engaging than jogging in a straight path. The long stairs leading to the Prison Ship Martyrs Monument is also a great exercise to start your day. Once you made it to the top, you will be rewarded to see beautiful plaza And if you're too tired, walking is also a great way to enjoy the park's scenery, take in fresh air and keep the body active. For those who want to relax, they can find a peaceful spot in the park, grab a blanket to lay on, and enjoy the serene surroundings.


Besides from the activities listed so far, you can also visit the park to hang out with your friends and families. You can find multiple playgrounds throughout the park, so if you need to babysitting a child consider bringing them along. If you are planning a family event and need a space, you can apply for permission to host in the barbeque area at Fort Greene Park right next to the main enterance. Ultimately, the park offers an array of activities that cater to different interests and preferences. Plan your visit today!

Park Events

Join the Birding: Beginner Birding event this Sunday, May 28, 2023. Fort Greene's Urban Park Rangers will guide you to the best viewing spots in New York City to see spring migratory birds. Birding programs are appropriate for all skill levels and beginners are welcome. Please bring your own binoculars. Join the Urban Park Rangers on a birding adventure around Fort Greene Park to learn the basics of birding how to identify common species in our parks. Register here.


Join the History by the Fireside event on June 10, 2023. Gather around the fire and learn our young nation's history with pirates. One of the overlooked consequences of American independence was the loss of British naval protection. Pirates of the Barbary stole goods and imprisoned sailors from the United States, leading to the creation of the US Navy. June 10th celebrates the anniversary of Yusuf Karamanli signing a treaty ending the First Barbary Wars. Register here.
